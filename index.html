Super Benji is a multimedia franchise featuring video games, comics, and an animated movie series created by themadpunter and triangle5820. The story follows the League of Grace, a heroic team led by Benji, as they battle against the villainous Dr. Banana and the Banana Republic.

Watch the Super Benji Animated Series

The Super Benji animated series, created using Scratch by triangle5820, presents the full story of Super Benji in an engaging and entertaining way. This series lays the foundation for the movie trilogy, showcasing Benji’s battles to stop Dr. Banana’s plans for world domination.

Watch the Super Benji Movie Series

Super Benji Movie

The first Super Benji Movie compiles the entire Super Benji Show into a full-length animated film. It follows Benji’s quest to reclaim the powerful Banana Gem from Dr. Banana. The series originally aired from December 4, 2021, to February 4, 2023. The movie version was released on February 25, 2023, with a runtime of 1 hour and 19 minutes.


Super Benji Movie 2: Civil War

Released on October 13, 2023, Super Benji Movie 2: Civil War continues the story immediately after the events of the Super Benji Show. In this sequel, Dr. Banana manipulates the League of Grace, causing them to split into two rival factions—one of which allies with the Banana Republic. The resulting conflict escalates into a full-scale civil war. Total runtime: 1 hour and 23 minutes.


Super Benji Movie 3: The Last Grace

The final movie in the Super Benji trilogy, Super Benji Movie 3: The Last Grace, was released on August 17, 2024, with a runtime of 1 hour and 46 minutes. This movie marks the end of triangle5820’s involvement in animation for the series, with future animation handled by themadpunter. The plot focuses on Dr. Banana’s minion, Minion, who creates a powerful artificial intelligence—Minion AI—and convinces it to help him conquer the world.


Watch the Super Benji Spinoff Series

Banana: A Super Benji Series

Banana: A Super Benji Series is a prequel focusing on Dr. Banana and his crew. This series explores Dr. Banana’s backstory, his rise to power, and the early formation of the Banana Republic. The series is ongoing and expected to feature a similar number of episodes as the original Super Benji Show.

Cooper: A Super Benji Series

Cooper: A Super Benji Series is set after the Super Benji movie trilogy. It follows Cooper as he works to rebuild the city and correct the damage left behind by both the Banana Republic and the League of Grace. This series also features the return of Minion AI, though it comes back without any memories of past events.
