Super Benji is a video game, comic, and movie series created by themadpunter and triangle5820. It stars the League of Grace, a team of crime-fighters led by Benji, as they battle the evil Dr. Banana and the Banana Republic.

Watch the Animated Series

The Super Benji animated series was created using Scratch, covering Benji's fight against Dr. Banana to stop his plan for world domination.

Super Benji Movie

The story of Benji reclaiming the Banana Gem from Dr. Banana. Compiled from the Super Benji Show episodes, this movie runs 1 hour, 19 minutes. Released February 25, 2023.

Super Benji Movie 2: Civil War

Dr. Banana manipulates the League of Grace into splitting apart, causing a full-on civil war. Released October 13, 2023. Runtime: 1 hour, 23 minutes.

Super Benji Movie 3: The Last Grace

Minion creates Minion AI, a powerful artificial intelligence bent on taking over the world. Released August 17, 2024. Runtime: 1 hour, 46 minutes.
