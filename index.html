Super Benji Official Site

Super Benji is an video game, comic, and movie series created by Themadpunter and triangle5820, starring the League of Grace, a team of crime-fighters led by their leader, Benji, as they fight to stop the evil Dr. Banana and the Banana Republic.

Watch the Animated Series

The Super Benji animated series was created by triangle5820 using Scratch as an animation platform to cover the story of Super Benji in a more entertaining way. The movie and show trilogy covers Benji's adventure stopping Dr. Banana from taking over the world.

Watch the Movie Series

Super Benji Movie

The Super Benji Show is the first animated series containing Super Benji. This story follows the story of Benji as he aims to reclaim the Banana Gem from the evil Dr. Banana. The first episode of the Super Benji Show was released on December 4, 2021, with the final episode releasing on February 4, 2023. The Super Benji Show was compiled into the first movie on February 25, 2023, with a total runtime of 1 hour, 19 minutes.


Super Benji Movie 2: Civil War

The second movie, Super Benji Movie 2: Civil War, was released on October 13, 2023, with a runtime of 1 hour, 23 minutes. This movie takes place shortly after the Super Benji Show, continuing its plot. In this movie, Dr. Banana tricks the League of Grace into splitting into two groups, with one of them working with the Banana Republic. This causes political tensions to escalate into all-out war.


Super Benji Movie 3: The Last Grace

The third movie released on August 17, 2024, with a total runtime of 1 hour, 46 minutes. This movie is the last movie in the Super Benji Movie trilogy, with the creator stating he will not be making a fourth movie, with animation efforts being taken over by Themadpunter. In this movie, Dr. Banana's underling, Minion, makes an artificial intelligence, Minion AI, that he convinces to help him take over the world.
